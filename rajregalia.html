About Developer

Amrut Laxmi Developers is a partnership firm currently developing the project Raj Regalia in Ambernath. The firm is currently undertaking the development of Phase 1 of the project. The firm’s activities are being managed by highly qualified professionals who are fully engrossed to ensure that the company maintains high standards in quality construction, timely delivery and customer satisfaction.
