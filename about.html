Bricking Your Dreams Since 2006

We at Raj Housing believe that everyone deserves to find their dream home

Home is where love resides, memories are made, laughter echoes and happiness is shared. Keeping this in mind, Naveen Bansal established Raj Housing, a group of real estate companies in 2006 with the purpose of providing people their dream home. Mr. Bansal, with more than 10 years of experience in his field knows the right blend of materials which make a perfect home. Raj Housing brings what people envisage to reality with world class construction crew, materials and equipment. Keeping in mind the needs of modern families, our houses have been carefully crafted with access to all amenities. We believe in providing our customers the best and we will be there to assist you through the whole process of buying a house.
